Technology Choices

The page or area describing the choices available to us through or by your chosen technology/topic. When we talk about choices, it clearly puts an individual at a disposition of answering a question. Like the question does Levi Ackerman from Attack on Titan actually die or nah.

The best feeling to a choice is when an individual comes up to you and asks a question. Questions like these are expressed via various way; some verbally, some orally, and etc.

Let me give you some examples of some Questions. Does Levi survive or does he meet his unfortunate defeat? Why is One Piece sooooo long? Why is MahiTahi a core paper for Bachelor of Computer and Information Science?

These questions may never be answered but for whoever is reading this website shall do the honours of completing these incomplete Questions

for me.
